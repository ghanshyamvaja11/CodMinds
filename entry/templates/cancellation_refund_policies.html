Cancellation & Refund Policies

1. Cancellation Policy

At CodMinds, we understand that circumstances can change. Below are our cancellation policies:

  • Cancellations made within 2-3 woeking days of enrollment are eligible for a full refund.
  • Cancellations made after 2-3 woeking days but before the course start date will incur a 10% administrative fee.
  • No cancellations or refunds will be processed once the course has started.

2. Refund Policy

Our refund policy is designed to ensure fairness to both the learners and CodMinds:

  • Refund requests must be submitted in writing to our support team.
  • Eligible refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days after approval.
  • Refunds are processed to the original mode of payment used during enrollment.
